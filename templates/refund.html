Return, Refund, Replacement and Cancellation Policy

Returns and Refunds:

To maintain quality and integrity of our merchandise, return of products or services shall not entitle refund from us. However, as our commitment is focused towards providing the best possible customer service, we facilitate immediate attention of the issue and replacement of products or components if applicable and the best resolution possible.

Replacement:

If a customer receives a product with any manufacturing defect or any other functional defect, the customer will be eligible for a replacement of such product, within the product’s service warranty period. The warranty period for our product is specified in the product’s documentation or description. To initiate such a replacement, the customer may have to contact our customer support team and provide the necessary details and evidence of such defect. We will promptly assess such request and, if approved, arrange for a replacement product. The replacement will be of the same product or an equivalent one, based on availability. Our aim is to ensure provisioning a defect-free, functional product.

Cancellation:

For product or service cancellations, we understand that circumstances may change for our customers. If our customers wish to cancel a product or service order, they can do so without incurring any cost to our company. Cancellations shall be made before shipment of products, or initiation of a service. To initiate such a cancellation, please contact our customer support team. Any payments made for canceled products or services will be refunded in a timely manner, using the original payment method. For products that are already shipped or services that are already initiated, our cancellation policy will not apply. We value our customers’ satisfaction and are committed to do our best to address any concern or issue that may arise during our customers’ interactions with our products or services. We are committed in delivering high-quality products and services to meet their requirements. Our customer support team will be always ready to assist them in the best possible manner.
