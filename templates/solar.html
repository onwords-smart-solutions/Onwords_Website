FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I install solar panels on any type of roof?

Yes, but installation methods vary based on roof type. Flat and sloped roofs have different mounting systems.

What is the average cost of installing solar panels?

Costs vary widely depending on capacity and quality, typically ranging from INR 60,000 to INR 80,000 per kW

Are there any subsidies available for solar panel installation?

Yes, the government offers subsidies, which can cover up to 40% of the cost for residential installations.

How long does it take to install solar panels?

The installation process can take a few days to a week, depending on the system size and complexity.

Do I need permission from local authorities for installing solar panels?

Yes, you may need to obtain approval from local municipal authorities and your electricity distribution company.
